About Me

Since high school science has been my passion. I always enjoyed learning about the laws and mechanisms that make the world and life operate. This passion has led me to an undergraduate degree in Environmental Science, a Masters in Biotechnology, and now to programming. Where does programming fit into the picture?

While in grad school I learned about this thing called coding and how it is the engine behind our digital world. My interest was greatly piqued when reading about all the great things that people have done with it- especially this TED talk about early cancer detection! I hope to be able to bring my knowledge of science to the digital realm to contribute to great healthcare products and ultimately help save peoples lives.

I am currently a lab tech for a global pharmaceutical company. If you are curious about my day to day work, it is accurately depicted here.
